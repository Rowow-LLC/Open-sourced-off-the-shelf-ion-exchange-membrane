## What Counts as Verified

A build is verified when there is public evidence (photos, video, or measured data) that the membrane was fabricated from the open-source recipe by someone other than Rowow LLC. Failed builds are listed too when reported; they carry the same evidentiary weight for process documentation.

## Why This Page Exists

Independent replication is the strongest evidence an open-source hardware process actually works as documented. This log is the public record.
